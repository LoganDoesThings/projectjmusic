## 📄 Main Store Listing

### **App Title**

### **Short Description (80 characters max)**

### **Full Description (4000 characters max)**
JMusic is a lightweight, high-performance music player designed for your local audio library. Experience your music without distractions, ads, or tracking.

**Features:**
- **Local Library Management:** Effortlessly scan and organize your device's audio files.
- **Folder Support:** Browse and play music by folders, giving you full control over your organization.
- **Precision Audio Control:** Powered by the latest audio technology for high-fidelity sound.
- **Favorites & Playlists:** Quickly heart your favorite tracks and manage your queue.
- **Sleep Timer:** Drift off to sleep with your favorite tunes; the app will automatically stop playback for you.
- **Modern UI:** A clean, dark-themed interface designed for simplicity and ease of use.
- **Customizable FX:** Adjust playback speed and pitch for a personalized listening experience.

JMusic respects your privacy. All your music and settings stay on your device—forever.
