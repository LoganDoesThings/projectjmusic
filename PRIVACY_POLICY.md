# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 5, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **JMusic**. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information.

## 2. Information We Collect
**JMusic** is an offline-first music player. 
- **No Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
- **Local Audio Files:** The app requires access to your device's storage to play your local music files. This data remains on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.
- **Local Settings:** Preferences such as favorites, folders, and playback settings are stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services that collect your data.

## 4. Permissions
To function correctly, JMusic may request the following permissions:
- **Storage/Media Access:** To scan and play audio files from your device.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
